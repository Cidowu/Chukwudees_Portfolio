Ford GoBike System User Anlysis¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of the Ford GoBike system users in the the greater San Francisco Bay area. I wanted to understand their behaviour in terms of trevel time by daily and weekly travels, and other characteristics like age, gender, and user type.

Dataset Overview¶

After cleaning, There were 174,760 riders' information over a period of one month in the dataset with 11 features. The features listed below are the features that were of interest to me.

  • rider age
  • user type
  • member gender
  • start time of day
  • week

Distribution of user type¶

The user type are primarily made up of two groups,the Subscribers, and the Customers. The visualization below show that the Subsribers make up a large portion of the market, while the Customers make up a smaller portion.

Conclusion¶

Though the dataset provided some insight in to the age and gender distribution of the users, there are limits to how much can be derived from the insights. This is because the dataset is limited to one month of user information, which is not enough to derive a better understanding of user patterns and behaviour.
